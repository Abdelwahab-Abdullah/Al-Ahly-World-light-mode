Establishment

The establishment of the club and its beginning, Omar Lotfi Bey,the owner of the idea of establishing Al-Ahly Club Omar Lotfi Bey put forward the idea of establishing Al-Ahly Club in the first decade of the century,The idea was born in his mind during his presidency of the High School Students Club,which was established in 1905,

He considered that the establishment of the High School Students Club is primarily political,It was found that these students need a sports club that brings them together to spend leisure time and exercise,So he presented the idea of establishing the club to a group of his friends who were enthusiastic about the idea They were headed by Mustafa Kamel,Al-Ahly was founded in 1907 and Al-Ahly is considered the first club for Egyptians in Egypt.
